Our Story

Immortal Being began as a private group founded in 2022. For four years it existed purely for fun — until April 23, 2026, when Shinez Kinji, as Founder, began developing it into a much larger community.

Community Rules
  • 01

    Respect every member without exception — zero tolerance for hate speech, racism, or discrimination.

  • 02

    Spam or unsolicited promotion without admin approval is strictly forbidden.

  • 03

    Maintain respectful language — this is a safe space for everyone.

  • 04

    Never share or leak private information of fellow members.

  • 05

    Follow admin directives — admin decisions are final.

  • 06

    Content involving SARA, pornography, or violence is strictly prohibited.

  • 07

    Use the Immortal Being name only for positive purposes.

Anyone who tarnishes the reputation of this community — especially through discrimination or misusing the name Immortal Being — will never be considered a part of us.

The Codex · Chapter 04

The Ancient Texts.

Lore, manifesto, and the sacred doctrine of Immortal Being.

I
Origin
The Birth of Immortal Being
In the year 2022, from the shadows of obscurity, a covenant was formed. Not out of necessity, but out of a shared hunger — a hunger to rise above, to build something that outlasts the ordinary. What began as a private circle of kindred spirits quietly grew, bound not by obligation but by blood and vision.

On April 23, 2026, the veil was lifted. Under the founding hand of Shinez Kinji, Immortal Being stepped from the shadows into its true form — a realm where those who dare to rise are given a home.
"We did not seek an audience. We sought brothers. We found an empire." — Shinez Kinji, Founder
II
Doctrine
The Seven Tenets
These are the pillars upon which every Immortal Being member stands. To break one is to break your oath.
I
HonorRespect all members of the realm without exception. We are one blood.
II
SilenceWhat is spoken within the realm stays within. No secrets shall be carried out.
III
OrderThe admins are the guardians. Their word is the law of the realm.
IV
PurityNo hatred, no discrimination, no poison. This realm is forged for the strong, not the hateful.
V
LegacyBear the name Immortal Being only in glory. We are defined by how we carry our name.
VI
AscensionWe do not stagnate. We grow, we build, we rise — always upward, never down.
VII
UnityA chain is only as strong as its weakest link. We lift each other or we fall together.
III
Vision
What We Are Building
Immortal Being is not merely a community. It is a movement — a proving ground for those who refuse mediocrity. We are building a space where talent is recognized, loyalty is rewarded, and ambition is celebrated.

The realm spans across Roblox, Discord, TikTok, YouTube, and beyond. Each platform is a different battlefield. Each battlefield is a chance to rise. And those who rise the highest will be immortalized in the Hall of Legends, remembered long after the game is over.
"Those who dare to rise shall never fall." — The Immortal Creed
